Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a session with Cueslife ?

Visit our Booking page, fill out the form with your details, select your preferred session type and time, and submit. You’ll receive a confirmation, and we’ll contact you to finalize the details. Note: You can book one session every 24 hours.

What types of sessions do you offer?

We offer Personal Training, Group Classes, Yoga Sessions, HIIT Workouts, Strength Training, Cardio , Pilates, and Online Coaching. Check our Services section for details.

Can I cancel or reschedule my booking?

Yes, contact us via the Contact form or WhatsApp (linked on the Booking page) at least 24 hours before your session to cancel or reschedule. Late cancellations may be subject to a fee.

Why can’t I book another session right away?

To ensure fair access, we limit bookings to one per person every 24 hours. You’ll see a message indicating when you can book again. If you need assistance, reach out via our Contact form.

Do you offer nutrition plans?

Yes, our Nutrition Plans are customized to your goals. Book a Personal Training or Online Coaching session to discuss your plan with a trainer.

What is your refund policy?

Refunds are handled case-by-case. Please review our Terms of Service or contact us for details.

How do I ask a question?

Use the “Have a Question?” form in the footer of any page to submit your question, or use the Contact form for detailed inquiries.

Is my personal information secure?

We prioritize your privacy. See our Privacy Policy for details on how we protect your data.
